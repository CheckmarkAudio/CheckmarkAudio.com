# Checkmark Audio School External Site Pathway

The studio website may mention Checkmark Audio School, but the school is its own separate website/project. CheckmarkAudio.com should stay studio-first and should only provide a short pathway or link-out to the school.

## Public Message We Can Use Now

Checkmark Audio School is a 12-month / 48-week studio-based program. Students learn by doing real audio work in a professional studio, moving from original production to recording, mixing, mastering, and final delivery.

## CTA Options

- `Learn Audio Engineering`.
- `Visit Checkmark Audio School`.
- `Book a School Admissions Call`.
