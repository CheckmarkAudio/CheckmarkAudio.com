# Checkmark Audio School Syllabus

## Course Overview

Checkmark Audio School is a 12-month, hands-on audio program taught in a professional studio environment. Students move from original production to studio recording to mixing, mastering, and final delivery.

## Course Length

- Duration: 12 months / 48 weeks
- Modules: 3
- Module length: 4 months / 16 weeks each
- Chapters per module: 8
- Chapter length: 2 weeks
- Classes per chapter: 2
- Class frequency: 1 class per week
- Class length: 4 hours
- Total classes: 48

## Course Modules

| Module | Duration | Chapters | Classes | Focus |
| --- | --- | ---: | ---: | --- |
| Module 1 | 4 months / 16 weeks | 8 | 16 | Music Production & Creative Sound Design |
| Module 2 | 4 months / 16 weeks | 8 | 16 | Studio Recording & Audio Engineering |
| Module 3 | 4 months / 16 weeks | 8 | 16 | Mixing, Mastering & Delivery |

## Learning Outcomes

By the end of the program, students will be able to:

- Produce an original song from scratch.
- Build an arrangement modeled from a professional reference track.
- Record vocals and instruments using microphones, preamps, and studio signal flow.
- Edit, comp, and organize audio sessions professionally.
- Mix using balance, panning, EQ, compression, effects, automation, and reference tracks.
- Master and deliver a final track at an appropriate technical standard.
- Explain and repeat a professional audio workflow.

## Student Deliverables

- Module 1: Fully arranged in-the-box production session
- Module 2: Fully tracked and edited recording session
- Module 3: Mixed, mastered, and presented final song

## Assessment Categories

- Class participation and studio professionalism
- Chapter assignments
- Skills checks
- Module final projects
- Final portfolio presentation

## Required Materials

Students need access to a DAW, headphones, storage for session files, and any required class materials assigned by the instructor. Starter Pack requirements and options are maintained in `../../operations/starter-pack-options.md`.

## Attendance Expectations

Because this program is hands-on and studio-based, attendance directly affects student progress. Missed classes must be handled according to `../late-work-and-makeup-policy.md`.

## Studio Expectations

Students are expected to treat the studio, equipment, teachers, guests, and other students with professional respect. Studio rules are maintained in `../studio-safety-policy.md` and `../equipment-use-policy.md`.
