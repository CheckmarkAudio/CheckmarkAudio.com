# Scope And Sequence

## Timeline Standard

- Full program: 12 months / 48 weeks
- Modules: 3
- Module length: 4 months / 16 weeks
- Chapters per module: 8
- Chapter length: 2 weeks
- Classes per chapter: 2
- Class frequency: 1 class per week
- Class length: 4 hours
- Total classes: 48
