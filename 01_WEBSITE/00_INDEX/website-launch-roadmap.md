# Checkmark Audio Website Launch Roadmap

## Publish Soon With Information We Have Now

These items can be built from the current playbook and curriculum files, using temporary "starting at" or "request a quote" language where exact pricing is not yet approved.

### Core Sales Pages

- Home page: explain Checkmark Audio as Albuquerque's professional music production studio for recording, mixing, mastering, artist development, music education, and creative media.
- Services page: group services into recording, production, mixing/mastering, artist media, and education.
- Booking page: offer clear paths for free consultation, recording session, studio rental, mixing inquiry, mastering inquiry, production inquiry, photography inquiry, school admissions call, and general contact.
- Contact page: build the layout and fields now, with final address/phone/email treated as approval blockers.
- About page: frame Checkmark as a professional music ecosystem in Albuquerque, connecting studio services, artist development, and education.
- School overview section/page: use existing curriculum content to describe the 12-month / 48-week audio program without letting the school overtake the studio site.
- FAQ page: draft high-intent questions around booking, pricing, deposits, studio prep, file delivery, revisions, and school enrollment.
- Policies page shell: create sections for deposit, cancellation, no-show, refund, revisions, file delivery, client responsibility, studio behavior, privacy, and terms.

### Conversion Sections

- Main CTA system: `Book a Free Consultation`, `View Services & Pricing`, and `Hear Our Work`.
- Service cards: service name, short description, best for, starting price or quote status, included items, and booking button.
- Pricing preview: cards can be structured now even if exact numbers are pending.
- Studio comparison section: Studio A vs. Studio B table structure can be built now, with final photos/prices/gear details pending.
- Footer: logo, short studio description, navigation, services, school link, policies, and contact placeholders.

### SEO Foundations

- Create clean slugs for core pages: `/`, `/services`, `/pricing`, `/book-now`, `/studios`, `/our-work`, `/school`, `/about`, `/contact`, `/faq`, `/policies`.
- Draft SEO titles and meta descriptions around Albuquerque studio intent.
- Add H1/H2/H3 hierarchy for every page.
- Add internal links from services to booking, pricing to booking, portfolio to services, and school to admissions.
- Prepare local keyword pages for later publishing: recording studio, vocal recording, mixing and mastering, music production, band recording, podcast recording, artist photography, and audio engineering school in Albuquerque.

### School Content We Can Use Now

- Program length: 12 months / 48 weeks.
- Format: 3 modules, 4 months each, 16 weeks each, 8 chapters each, 48 total classes.
- Core promise: students learn by doing real audio work in a professional studio with teacher guidance, critique, repetition, and clear deliverables.
- Student outcomes: production, recording, editing, mixing, mastering, delivery, and repeatable professional workflow.
- Final deliverable: a mixed, mastered, portfolio-ready song.

## Essential Next Additions That Need More Content

These are required for a fully credible launch, but they need approved written content, image content, accurate business info, or access to systems.

- Final contact details: address, ZIP, phone, email, hours, map link, Google Business Profile link, and social links.
- Approved pricing: studio time, recording, mixing, mastering, production, band recording, photography, visualizers, EPK, and school/admissions pricing or inquiry rules.
- Approved booking workflow: booking platform, consultation flow, calendar access, deposit behavior, confirmation message, and correct recipient email.
- Current policies: deposit, cancellation, no-show, refund, mix/master revision, file delivery, client responsibility, studio behavior, privacy, and terms.
- Current team list: who is public-facing, their roles, bios, photos, services handled, rates if applicable, and booking links.
- Studio A content: current photos, best uses, gear highlights, vocal booth details, control room details, band/acoustic capability, and starting price.
- Studio B content: current photos, best uses, booth details, ideal session type, gear highlights, and starting price.
- Portfolio proof: audio embeds, video embeds, Checkmark Live Sessions, before/after mix examples, artist photos, credits, genres, and project descriptions.
- Testimonials: client reviews tied to real services or projects where possible.
- Final brand approvals: final logo set, colors, fonts, image style, and any non-negotiable brand language.
