## Completion Standard From The Playbook

The website is not complete until a first-time visitor can understand:

- What Checkmark Audio does.
- Who the studio is for.
- What services are available.
- What the starting prices are.
- Why they should trust Checkmark.
- How to book or contact Checkmark.
- Where Checkmark is located.
- What the work sounds and looks like.
- How Checkmark Audio School connects to the studio.
- How to take the next step without confusion.

## How To Use This Checklist

- `Answered` means the playbook item has enough information from the live site, repo, or user input.
- `Drafted` means we have a proposed answer that still needs approval.
- `Needs Bridget/Gavin` means the answer requires business info, media, pricing, approval, or access.
- `Later` means it belongs after the core launch.
