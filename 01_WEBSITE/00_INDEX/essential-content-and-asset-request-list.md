# Essential Content And Asset Request List

## Approvals Needed First

- Final business name formatting.
- Correct address, ZIP, phone, email, hours, and response-time expectation.
- Google Business Profile link.
- Instagram, Facebook, YouTube, TikTok, Spotify, SoundCloud, or other active social links.
- Current team list and who should appear publicly.
- Current service list and any services that should not be promoted yet.
- Current pricing and quote rules.
- Deposit, cancellation, no-show, refund, revision, file delivery, and studio behavior policies.
- Booking platform or preferred booking/contact flow.

## Visual Assets Needed

- Final Checkmark Audio logo in light, dark, transparent, and square/social formats.
- Current exterior/location photo if useful for first-time visitors.
- Studio A hero photo, booth photo, control room photo, gear photo, and session photo.
- Studio B hero photo, booth photo, writing/session photo, and gear photo.
- Team headshots.
- Client/session photos cleared for web use.
- Artist photography examples.
- Cover art, lyric video, visualizer, and EPK examples if those services are being sold.

## Portfolio Assets Needed

- Embedded audio examples.
- Embedded videos.
- Checkmark Live Sessions examples.
- Before/after mix examples.
- Credits: artist, song/project, service performed, engineer/producer, genre, and release link.
- Student work examples approved for public use.
- Testimonials connected to real projects where possible.

## Written Content Needed

- Homepage hero headline approval.
- About story: origin, mission, Albuquerque community role, professional standards, and education connection.
- Team bios.
- Studio A description.
- Studio B description.
- Service descriptions.
- Pricing card copy.
- FAQ answers.
- Policy copy.
- Confirmation message for forms.
- School admissions CTA copy.

## Data Consistency Checks

The same business details must match everywhere:

- Website header and footer.
- Contact page.
- Booking page.
- Google Business Profile.
- Yelp.
- Instagram.
- Facebook.
- School website/page.
- Sitemap and structured data.
