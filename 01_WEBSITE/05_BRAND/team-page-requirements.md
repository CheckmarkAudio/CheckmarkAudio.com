# Team Page Requirements

The team page must be current and accurate. No outdated staff should remain public.

## Each Team Member Needs

- Name.
- Role.
- Photo.
- Short bio.
- Best for.
- Services they handle.
- Starting rate, if applicable.
- Book with this person button, if applicable.
