# Pricing Page Requirements

The pricing page should be clean, direct, and easy to scan.

## Must Include

- Studio time rates.
- Recording rates.
- Mixing rates.
- Mastering rates.
- Production rates.
- Band recording rates.
- Photography rates.
- Lyric video / visualizer rates.
- School pricing or link to school pricing.
- Consultation option.
- Deposit policy.
- Cancellation policy.
- Disclaimer that custom quotes may vary.

## Pricing Card Fields

- Service name.
- Price.
- Session length or deliverable.
- What is included.
- Best for.
- Book button.

## Content Needed

- Approved current pricing.
- Deposit policy.
- Cancellation policy.
- Custom quote language.
