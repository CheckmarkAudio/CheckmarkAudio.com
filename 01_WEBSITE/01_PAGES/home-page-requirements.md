# Home Page Blueprint

The homepage is the main sales page. It should help a first-time visitor understand what Checkmark Audio does, who it helps, why to trust the studio, what to click next, and how the school fits into the larger ecosystem.

## Page Goal

Convert first-time visitors into one of four actions:

- Book a session.
- View services and pricing.
- Hear or see proof of the work.
- Explore the school pathway.

## Audience Positioning

Primary audience: music artists, singers, and bands.

Secondary audience: anyone who needs professional recording, mixing, or mastering, including voice-over artists, streamers, filmmakers, podcast-style clients, and other creative audio clients.

## Primary KPI

Homepage `Book a Session` clicks.

## Secondary KPIs

- Services/pricing clicks.
- Portfolio play/clicks.
- Tap-to-call or tap-to-text clicks on mobile.
- School pathway clicks.
- Contact/location clicks.
